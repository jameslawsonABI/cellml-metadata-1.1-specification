Examples

We should use less examples and make them more meaningful - specific to CellML, that is.

E.g. for DC, we could have an example citation which combines the current examples (such as publisher, contributor etc. etc.) If people want to learn how to use DC, that's not really our prerogative, they should go to the DC site.

Similarly, I don' t plan to include any of the RDF examples or explanation from the 1.0 spec since that spec was written at a time when RDF (and particularly RDF/XML) was a fledgling technology. There's no reason to clutter our spec with examples of how to implement standards that are much wider and better defined than CellML Metadata.
